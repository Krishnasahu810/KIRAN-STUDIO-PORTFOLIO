About Kiran Studio

The Art of Seeing Behind the Lens

At Kiran Studio, we believe every moment deserves to be remembered — from the grandest wedding to the quietest newborn breath.

We specialize in wedding photography, pre-wedding shoots, haldi & mehendi functions, maternity sessions, baby & newborn shoots, and family portraits. Based in Dabra, Gwalior, we travel across Madhya Pradesh and beyond to document your most precious milestones with cinematic artistry and a genuine heart.
